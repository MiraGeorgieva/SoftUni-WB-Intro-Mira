About the course

What to expect

8 weeks of hard work, confusion, regret, desperation and proud feeling of achievement (in the exact order)

Sudden craving for home cooked food, clean house, garage, and attic, charity work, visit to distant relatives, pretty much anything over doing actual WEB fundamentals work in your only spare time window.

For all

Accessibility

What does "Accessibility" mean?

Accessibility means making the profession accessible to people with certain challenges.

For example, people who use Accessibility software to read web pages. It is important to optimize our content towards such software.

It is also important to add optimized captions to our videos for those who cannot hear the lectures.

What can we do?

We can have it in mind while creating pages.

We can advise our clients and colleagues on how to thoughtfully optimize their produce.

We can live a more appreciative life.

All good things come to an end.

Thank you for sharing your knowledge!
